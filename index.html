How to get HBO Max as an Oregon State University student

  1. Visit hbomax.com and click "Sign In" in the top-right corner.

  2. In the pop-up window click "Sign In With a Provider"

  3. From the list of providers click on "xfinity"

  4. Where it says "Xfinity On Campus students" click "Sign in here"

  5. In the searchbox type in "Oregon State University" then click "Submit".

  6. Sign in using your ONID credentials.

  7. Confirm your login using the Duo mobile app.
